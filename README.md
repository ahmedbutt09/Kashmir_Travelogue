🏔️ Kashmir Travelogue

A beautifully designed travelogue project showcasing the breathtaking beauty of Azad Jammu & Kashmir (Pakistan). This project highlights major tourist destinations, cultural richness, and natural landscapes through engaging content and visuals.

🌄 Overview

The Kashmir Travelogue project is created to promote tourism and provide detailed information about famous destinations in Azad Kashmir. It serves as a digital guide for travelers, students, and tourism enthusiasts.

📍 Featured Destinations
Neelum Valley
Leepa Valley
Pir Chinasi
Toli Peer
Banjosa Lake
Ganga Choti

Each location includes descriptions, scenic highlights, and travel insights.

✨ Features
Informative travel content
Destination-based sections
Visual representation of scenic spots
Easy navigation through locations
Focus on tourism awareness and cultural promotion
